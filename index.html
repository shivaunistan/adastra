Welcome to Ad Astra Comix.

Comics have become a unique medium by which we can explore ideas. At a time when the future of the printed page seems precarious, comics and graphic novels are experiencing a renaissance in both form and content. AD ASTRA COMIX is about using the medium to explore political ideas, with a focus on EDUCATION, PROMOTION, and PRODUCTION. Welcome the wonderful world of Politically Charged Graphic Words.
